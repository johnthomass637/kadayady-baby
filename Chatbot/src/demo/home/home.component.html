The Anthem Health Care Management Division has a singular dynamic focus - to continually improve the system of health care delivery that influences utilization and cost of services and measures performance. Medical Management includes a collection of processes that help to optimize the cost of quality care by effectively facilitating the right care interventions to the right members, at the right time, and in the right setting.

Utilization Management (UM) is a process that allows Anthem to manage the cost of health care benefits by assessing its appropriateness before it is provided, by using evidence-based criteria or guidelines.

Pre- authorization review which is done as part of UM requests by providers/practitioners of benefits are reviewed by Anthem registered nurses to determine if requests meet applicable benefit coverage and Anthem medical policy criteria.

While both Payer(Anthem) and Provider(Physicians) want to give good care for the patients, the providers have incentive to prescribe arguably redundant treatment to avoid future questioning and even mal-practice suits. On the other hand, the payer would like to pay only for the treatments that are necessary. This tradeoff is where the UM (Utilization management) system comes in.
